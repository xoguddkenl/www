PISIN MOLITO PATEL
A brave and wise person who survived the drift of 227 days. Currently, he has settled in Canada, raised a family, and teaches Judaism at university.He constantly tries to talk in a long drift. Conversation with himself through the diary, with God through prayer, and with Richard Parker the Tiger (in other versions of the story, Richard Parker was a pie, so a conversation with Richard Parker is nothing more than a conversation with himself). It is also a process of true reflection, discovering some power within oneself. In the process, problems of faith, something spiritual, problems of life and death, problems of truth and fantasy, etc. are raised.
